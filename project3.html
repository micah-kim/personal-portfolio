Augmented Museum App: A New Way to Visualize Art

Contributor(s): Micah Kim
Event: Personal Project
Personal Project
Dates: 07-15-2021 ~ 8-15-2021

I've always loved art but I was never a fan of those plaques that have the art's description. It ruin's the museum's neat asthetic and isn't good for the environment. By optimizing ARkit's image recognition software, I created an application that anchor's virtual text when detecting an artword through the phone's camera.
