Recycle It: Using Machine Learning to Identify Recyclable Items

Contributor(s): Micah Kim, Benjamin Lee, Andrew Choi
Event: Boston College Hack The Heights Hackathon 2021
Hackathon
Dates: 11-10-2021 ~ 11-11-2021

This project was completed for Boston College's 2021 Hack the Heights hackathon. By training our own machine learning model, my team and I engineered an iOS application that can detect different types of recyclable materials through the camera. I was primarily in charge of coding the Swift application and my teammates took charge in coding the UI and creating a presentation of our work. We used Swift, Apple's ML Model, Xcode, and ARKit for our project and ended up winning a placement in the competition.
