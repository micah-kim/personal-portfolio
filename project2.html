Recycle It: Using Machine Learning to Identify Recyclable Items

Contributor(s): Micah Kim, Benjamin Lee, Andrew Choi
Event: Boston College Hack The Heights Hackathon 2021
Purpose: Sustainability Initiative
Dates: 11-10-2021 ~ 11-11-2021

Flann O'brien's novel The Third Policeman, published in 1939, is a dark comic novel exploring the nature of death, time, and existance. It is very impactful piece of Irish Literature is very well-known throughout the world. In this project, I worked with a team of students interested in Computer Science, English, and Film to recreate scenes from the novel in Augmented Reality. By Using the Swift programming language, optimizing the ARKit framework, creating 3D models using Cinema 4D, and animating them using Reality Composer. This project will be presented at the International Flann O'Brien Conference in April, 2022.
